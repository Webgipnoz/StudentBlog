Who are we?

We are a group of friends and colleagues who decided to help those people who will replace us within the walls of this wonderful institution. In our team, almost everyone is in their last years or has already received a diploma. But we continue to participate in the life of our favorite institution!

We hope that our work will really help you to study these wonderful 4 years here, you can find all the information yourself, but it's nice when they find it for you and serve it to you!
